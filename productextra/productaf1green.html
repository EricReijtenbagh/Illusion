  • The Nike Air Force 1 Low Off White Light Green Spark is the result of the collaboration between Off White and Nike Air Force. The shoes have striking green leather uppers with stitched-on metallic silver foil Swooshes on the midfoot. The look is completed by matching AF1 sole pieces bearing the word "AIR" in large letters, as well as by bigger text branding on the inner lateral part. The Nike Air Force 1 Low Off White Light Green Spark debuted on September 15, 2022, and retailed at $160.
